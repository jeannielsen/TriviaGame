Movie Trivia Game

This is a short movie trivia game. When the game begins you will be given 30 seconds to answer a movie trivia question by clicking on the correct answer. Once you have answered, the game will move on to a new question. If your answer is correct, you will be given a point. If the time runs out before you answer or if you choose an incorrect answer, you will not receive a point. The game begins when you push the “Start” button below.
